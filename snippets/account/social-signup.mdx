<Tabs>
<Tab title="Google" icon="chrome">
**Sign up with Google**

<Steps>
<Step title="Click Google signup">
  Click the **"Continue with Google"** button on the signup page.
</Step>

<Step title="Grant permissions">
  - Google will ask for permission to access your basic profile information -
  Review the permissions requested by Tars - Click **"Allow"** to grant access
</Step>

<Step title="Account creation">
  - Tars automatically creates your account using your Google profile - Your
  name and email are imported from Google - No additional information required
</Step>

<Step title="Access dashboard">
  - You're automatically signed in and redirected to your dashboard
  - Start creating your first AI Agent immediately
</Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
**Gmail & Google Workspace**: Works with both personal Gmail accounts and Google Workspace business accounts.
</Note>
</Tab>

<Tab title="SSO" icon="shield-check">
**Enterprise Single Sign-On**

<Steps>
<Step title="Click SSO signup">
  Click the **"Continue with SSO"** button on the signup page.
</Step>

<Step title="Select your organization">
  - Choose your organization from the list - Or enter your organization's SSO
  domain - Click **"Continue"**
</Step>

<Step title="Authenticate with your organization">
  - You'll be redirected to your organization's login page - Enter your
  corporate credentials - Complete any required two-factor authentication
</Step>

<Step title="Account creation">
  - Tars automatically creates your account using your SSO profile - Your
  corporate information is imported securely - Account is linked to your
  organization
</Step>

<Step title="Access dashboard">
  - You're automatically signed in and redirected to your dashboard
  - Start creating your first AI Agent immediately
</Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
**Enterprise Ready**: Secure integration with your organization's identity provider (SAML, OAuth, etc.).
</Note>
