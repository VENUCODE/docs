## Create from PDF Documents

**Upload and process PDF documents to build knowledge bases**

Transform your PDF documents into searchable knowledge bases that can answer questions and provide information from your document content.

### What you get

- **Text extraction** from PDF documents with high accuracy
- **Table processing** for structured data within PDFs
- **Multi-page support** for comprehensive document processing
- **Content organization** and automatic indexing
- **Search optimization** for quick information retrieval

### Perfect for

- **Product manuals** and technical documentation
- **Research papers** and academic content
- **Legal documents** and contracts
- **Training materials** and guides
- **Reports** and white papers

### Supported features

- **Multi-page documents** with automatic page processing
- **Table extraction** for structured data
- **Image text recognition** (OCR) for scanned documents
- **Metadata extraction** for document organization
- **Content chunking** for optimal AI processing
