Channel Settings allow you to connect and configure WhatsApp Business Numbers for your AI Agents, enabling seamless communication through WhatsApp messaging.

## WhatsApp Business Numbers Management

<Steps>
<Step title="Add Business Number" icon="plus">
  Click the `+ Add Business Number` button in the top-right corner to open the configuration modal.

    <Frame>
    <img src="/images/dashboard/account/whatsapp/step-1-click-add-bussiness-number.png" alt="WhatsApp Numbers page with Add Business Number button" />
    </Frame>

  <Frame>
  <img src="/images/dashboard/account/whatsapp/step-2-whatsapp-number-configuration.png" alt="Add Business Number modal with configuration form" />
  </Frame>
  
  **Required Information:**
  - **Business Number**: Enter your WhatsApp Business phone number with country code
  - **API Key**: Provide your WhatsApp Business API key for authentication
  - **About**: Brief description of the business
  - **Description**: Detailed description of the business purpose
  - **Company Name**: Your organization name
  - **Business Vertical**: Select your industry category
  - **Profile Picture**: Upload your business profile picture
  
  Click `Save` to add the business number to your account.
</Step>

<Step title="Business Number Added" icon="check">
  Once successfully added, your business number appears in the WhatsApp Numbers
  table.
  <Frame>
    <img
      src="/images/dashboard/account/whatsapp/step-3-added-WB-number.png"
      alt="WhatsApp Numbers table showing added business number"
    />
  </Frame>
  **Table Structure:** - **Business Number**: Shows your added WhatsApp number -
  **Agent**: Displays "Connect Agent" button for Agent assignment - **Actions**:
  Gear icon for additional options
  <Check>Your business number is now ready for Agent connection.</Check>
</Step>

<Step title="Connect Agent" icon="link">
  Click the `Connect Agent` button to assign an AI Agent to your WhatsApp
  number.
  <Frame>
    <img
      src="/images/dashboard/account/whatsapp/step-4-click-connect-agent.png"
      alt="Connect Agent button highlighted in the table"
    />
  </Frame>
  This opens the Agent selection modal where you can choose from your created
  agents.
</Step>

<Step title="Select and Connect Agent" icon="robot">
  In the Agent selection modal, choose the Agent you want to connect to your
  WhatsApp number.
  <Frame>
    <img
      src="/images/dashboard/account/whatsapp/step-5-select-agent-and-connect-Agent.png"
      alt="Agent selection modal with available agents"
    />
  </Frame>
  **Agent Selection:** - Browse through all your created agents - Use the search
  bar to find specific agents - Select the desired Agent (highlighted with
  purple border and checkmark) - Click `Connect Agent` to complete the
  connection
  <Info>
    **Agent Assignment**: The selected Agent will handle all conversations for
    this WhatsApp number.
  </Info>
</Step>

<Step title="Agent Connected Successfully" icon="circle-check">
  Once connected, your Agent is now active on the WhatsApp number.
  <Frame>
    <img
      src="/images/dashboard/account/whatsapp/step-6-agent-connected-to-whatapp-number.png"
      alt="Agent successfully connected to WhatsApp number with success notification"
    />
  </Frame>
  **Connection Confirmation:** - Agent name appears in the table under the
  "Agent" column - Success notification confirms the connection - Agent is now
  ready to handle WhatsApp conversations
  <Check>
    **Live Integration**: Customers can now message your WhatsApp number and
    interact with your AI Agent.
  </Check>
</Step>

<Step title="Disconnect Agent" icon="link-slash">
  To disconnect an Agent from your WhatsApp number, click the 'X' icon next to
  the Agent name.
  <Frame>
    <img
      src="/images/dashboard/account/whatsapp/step-7-click-disconnect-agent-button.png"
      alt="Disconnect Agent button with X icon highlighted"
    />
  </Frame>
  This opens a confirmation modal to ensure you want to remove the Agent
  connection.
</Step>

<Step title="Confirm Agent Removal" icon="trash">
  In the removal confirmation modal, review the action and confirm the
  disconnection.
  <Frame>
    <img
      src="/images/dashboard/account/whatsapp/step-8-click-remove-agent.png"
      alt="Remove Agent confirmation modal"
    />
  </Frame>
  **Removal Process:** - Review the confirmation message - Click `Remove Agent`
  to disconnect the Agent - The Agent will be disconnected from the WhatsApp
  number - You can now connect a different Agent if needed
  <Warning>
    **Agent Disconnection**: Once removed, the Agent will no longer respond to
    messages on this WhatsApp number.
  </Warning>
</Step>

<Step title="Manage WhatsApp Number" icon="gear">
  Click the gear icon in the Actions column to access additional options for your WhatsApp number.
  
  <Frame>
  <img src="/images/dashboard/account/whatsapp/step-9-whatsapp-number-quick-actions.png" alt="Quick actions dropdown with Edit and Delete options" />
  </Frame>
  
  **Available Actions:**
  - **Edit**: Modify business number details, API key, and other configuration
  - **Delete**: Remove the WhatsApp number completely from your account
  
  <Info>
  **Edit Functionality**: The edit option opens the same configuration modal where you can update all business number details.
  </Info>
</Step>
</Steps>
