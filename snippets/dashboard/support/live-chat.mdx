## Accessing live chat

<Steps>
<Step title="Click Live Chat" icon="headset">
  In the support dropdown menu, click on `Live Chat` to open the support chatbot widget.
  
  <Check>
  This opens the TARS AI Support chat widget on the right side of your screen.
  </Check>
</Step>

<Step title="Interact with support bot" icon="robot">
  Engage with the TARS AI Support chat widget to efficiently resolve your
  queries. For best results, provide detailed descriptions of your issues or
  questions. Utilize the voice input feature for convenience, and remember that
  the AI is designed to offer instant responses and escalate complex issues to
  human agents when necessary.
  <Info>
    **AI-powered support**: The chat widget is powered by AI to provide instant
    responses to common questions and can escalate complex issues to human
    support agents.
  </Info>
</Step>

<Step title="Get real-time assistance" icon="comments">
  Get immediate help with platform navigation, technical issues, and implementation guidance. The AI-powered chat provides instant responses and can escalate complex issues to human agents.
</Step>
</Steps>
