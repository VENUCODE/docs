## Accessing Video Tutorials

<Steps>
<Step title="Click Video Tutorials" icon="play">
  In the support dropdown menu, click on `Video Tutorials` to access our comprehensive video library.
  
  <Check>
  This opens the TARS YouTube channel in a new browser tab, keeping your current work open.
  </Check>
</Step>

<Step title="Explore YouTube Channel" icon="youtube">
  The TARS YouTube channel opens with access to: **Channel Features:** - **523+
  Videos**: Comprehensive library of tutorials and guides - **1.1K+
  Subscribers**: Active community of learners - **Regular Updates**: New content
  added regularly - **No-Code Focus**: Specialized content for building AI
  agents without coding
  <Info>
    **Channel Description**: "Build Agents in Minutes with Our No-Code Platform"
    - focused on empowering users to create AI solutions without technical
    expertise.
  </Info>
</Step>

<Step title="Find Relevant Content" icon="magnifying-glass">
  Use the YouTube channel features to find the content you need:
  
  **Search and Navigation:**
  - **Search Bar**: Use YouTube's search to find specific topics
  - **Playlists**: Browse organized collections of related videos
  - **Channel Tabs**: Explore different content types (Videos, Shorts, Live, Playlists)
  - **Subscribe**: Click the "Subscribed" button to get notifications for new content
  
  
  <Tip>
  **Stay Updated**: Subscribe to the channel to receive notifications when new tutorials are published.
  </Tip>
</Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  **Learning Path**: Start with "Getting Started" videos if you're new to the
  platform, then explore industry-specific content relevant to your use case.
</Tip>
