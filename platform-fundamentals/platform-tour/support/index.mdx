The Support section provides multiple channels to help you get the most out of your Tars platform experience. Access tutorials, get real-time assistance, explore templates, and connect with our community.

## Accessing Support

<Steps>
<Step title="Click Support Button" icon="circle-question">
  Navigate to the sidebar and click on the **Support** button to open the support options panel.
  
  <Frame>
  <img src="/images/dashboard/support/step-1-click-support.png" alt="Support button in left sidebar with dropdown menu open" />
  </Frame>
  
  <Check>
  This opens a quick access panel with all available support options and resources.
  </Check>
</Step>

<Step title="Select support option" icon="list">
  Choose from the available support options in the dropdown menu:
  
  <Frame>
  <img src="/images/dashboard/support/step-2-click-relevant-option.png" alt="Support dropdown menu showing all available options" />
  </Frame>
  
  **Available Support Options:**
  - **Video Tutorials**: Access comprehensive video guides and tutorials
  - **Live Chat**: Get real-time assistance from our support team
  - **Schedule a Demo**: Book a personalized demonstration
  - **AI Agent Templates**: Explore pre-built Agent templates
  - **Community**: Join our Discord community for discussions and support
  
  <Info>
  **New tab opening**: All support options open in new browser tabs, allowing you to keep your current work open while accessing support resources.
  </Info>
</Step>
</Steps>

## Best Practices

<Tip>
  **Start with Video Tutorials**: For common questions and learning, video
  tutorials often provide the quickest answers and step-by-step guidance.
</Tip>

<Warning>
  **Live Chat Availability**: Live chat support is available during business
  hours. For urgent issues outside these hours, consider using the community or
  scheduling a demo.
</Warning>

<Info>
  **Community Benefits**: The Discord community is a great place to connect with
  other users, share experiences, and get peer-to-peer support from experienced
  Tars users.
</Info>
