**Account & Team Management**

Profile Settings is your control center for account management, team collaboration, and platform preferences.

<Frame>
<img src="/images/profile-settings.png" alt="Profile settings dashboard showing account management options" />
</Frame>

**What you can do here:**
- **Manage Your Profile**: Update personal information, preferences, and security settings
- **Team Collaboration**: Invite team members and manage permissions
- **Billing & Subscriptions**: Handle payments, upgrade plans, and view usage
- **Platform Preferences**: Customize notifications, themes, and default settings

**Key subsections:**
- **Personal Profile**: Account information, avatar, and contact details
- **Team Management**: Invite members, assign roles, and manage permissions
- **Billing Center**: Subscription details, payment methods, and usage tracking
- **Security Settings**: Password management, two-factor authentication, and access logs
- **Preferences**: Notification settings, theme selection, and default configurations

**Available actions:**
- Update profile information
- Change password and security settings
- Invite team members
- Manage subscription and billing
- Configure notification preferences
- Set up two-factor authentication
- View account activity logs
- Export account data
</Tab>
