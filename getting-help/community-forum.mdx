## Getting started in the community

<Steps>
<Step title="Join Discord" icon="discord">
  Click the Discord invite link to join our main community server.
  
  <Check>
  **Welcome Channel**: Start by introducing yourself in the #welcome channel.
  </Check>
</Step>

<Step title="Follow Social Media" icon="share">
  Follow TARS on your preferred social platforms to stay updated with the latest news and features.
  
  <Tip>
  **Notifications**: Enable notifications for important announcements and updates.
  </Tip>
</Step>

<Step title="Introduce Yourself" icon="user">
  Share your background, use case, and goals with the community to connect with like-minded members.
  
  **What to Share:**
  - Your role and industry
  - How you're using TARS
  - What you hope to learn or achieve
  - Any specific challenges you're facing
</Step>

<Step title="Participate Actively" icon="comments">
  Engage in discussions, ask questions, and share your experiences to build meaningful connections.
  
  **Ways to Participate:**
  - Ask questions in relevant channels
  - Share your success stories
  - Help answer questions from other members
  - Provide feedback on new features
</Step>
</Steps>
