### How to sign in

- Enter your registered email address
- Enter your password
- Click **"Login"** to access your account

## Forgot your password?

If you can't remember your password, follow these steps to reset it:

<Steps>
<Step title="Request password reset">
  - Click **"Forgot Password?"** on the login page
  - Enter your email address
  - Click **"Send Reset Link"**
</Step>

<Step title="Check your email">
  - Look for the reset email in your inbox
  - Click the reset link in the email
</Step>

<Step title="Create new password">
  - You'll be redirected to the password reset page
  - Enter your **New Password**
  - **Confirm New Password**
  - Click **"Reset Password"**
</Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  **Check your spam folder** if you don't see the reset email within a few minutes.
</Warning>

## Security tips

<Tip>
  **Use a strong password** with at least 8 characters, including uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Enable two-factor authentication** in your account settings for extra security.
</Tip>
