# Account Setup Guide

Get started with Tars by creating your account or logging in using your preferred authentication method. This guide covers all available signup and login options.

## Creating Your Tars Account

### Quick Registration

Create your Tars account in just a few simple steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Signup Page">
    Go to the Tars registration page to begin account creation.
    
    ### Required Information
    You'll need to provide:
    - **First Name**: Your given name
    - **Last Name**: Your surname
    - **Work Email**: Your professional email address
    - **Phone Number**: Including country code (e.g., +91 for India)
    
    <Note>
      Use your work email address for better team collaboration and professional account management.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose Your Signup Method">
    Select how you want to create your account.
    
    ### Manual Registration
    Fill out the registration form with your details:
    1. Enter your **First Name** and **Last Name**
    2. Provide your **Work Email** address
    3. Select your **Country** from the dropdown (defaults to your location)
    4. Enter your **Phone Number** with the correct country code
    5. Click **"Start my free trial now"**
    
    ### Social Authentication
    Alternatively, sign up instantly using:
    
    <Columns cols={2}>
      <Card
        title="Google Account"
        icon="chrome"
      >
        Sign up with your Google/Gmail account for instant access
      </Card>
      <Card
        title="LinkedIn Account"
        icon="linkedin"
      >
        Use your LinkedIn profile for professional account setup
      </Card>
      <Card
        title="Outlook Account"
        icon="envelope"
      >
        Sign up with your Microsoft/Outlook account
      </Card>
      <Card
        title="SSO Integration"
        icon="shield-check"
      >
        Use Single Sign-On if your organization supports it
      </Card>
    </Columns>
    
    <Check>
      Your account is created immediately when using social authentication methods.
    </Check>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete Account Verification">
    Verify your account and complete the setup process.
    
    ### Email Verification
    1. Check your email inbox for a verification message from Tars
    2. Click the verification link in the email
    3. Your account will be activated automatically
    
    ### Initial Setup
    After verification, you'll be guided through:
    - **Workspace configuration**
    - **Use case selection** (Customer Support, Lead Generation, etc.)
    - **Platform tour** (highly recommended for new users)
    
    <Tip>
      Complete the platform tour to get hands-on experience and create your first agent automatically.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Logging Into Your Account

### Standard Login Process

Access your existing Tars account using multiple authentication methods:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Login Page">
    Visit the Tars login page to access your account.
    
    ### Login Credentials
    Enter your account information:
    - **Email Address**: The email you used during registration
    - **Password**: Your account password
    
    ### Forgot Password?
    If you've forgotten your password:
    1. Click **"Forgot Password?"** on the login page
    2. Enter your email address
    3. Check your email for password reset instructions
    4. Follow the link to create a new password
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose Your Login Method">
    Select your preferred authentication method.
    
    ### Email & Password Login
    The traditional login method:
    1. Enter your **Email Address**
    2. Enter your **Password**
    3. Click **"Login"** to access your account
    
    ### Social Authentication Login
    Quick access using your existing accounts:

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

### Login Problems

<Accordion title="Can't remember which authentication method I used">
  If you're unsure how you originally signed up:
  1. Try your email and password first
  2. If that doesn't work, try the social authentication buttons
  3. Look for welcome emails from Tars to identify your signup method
  4. Contact support if you're still unable to access your account
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Social authentication not working">
  If social login fails:
  1. Ensure you're using the same account you used for signup
  2. Check if your browser is blocking popups
  3. Try clearing your browser cache and cookies
  4. Verify your social account (Google, LinkedIn, etc.) is still active
  5. Try using a different browser or incognito mode
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Email verification issues">
  If you haven't received the verification email:
  1. Check your spam/junk folder
  2. Wait a few minutes - emails can be delayed
  3. Try requesting a new verification email
  4. Ensure you entered the correct email address during signup
  5. Contact support if the issue persists
</Accordion>

### Account Access

<Accordion title="Account locked or suspended">
  If your account appears to be locked:
  1. Check for any security-related emails from Tars
  2. Ensure you haven't exceeded login attempt limits
  3. Verify your account is in good standing (billing, terms compliance)
  4. Contact Tars support for account status review
</Accordion>
