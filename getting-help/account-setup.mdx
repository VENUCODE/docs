## Create your account

Choose your preferred signup method to get started with Tars.

<Frame>
<img src="/images/signup.png" alt="Tars account creation page showing signup form and social login options" />
</Frame>

### Signup options

<Tabs>
<Tab title="Manual signup">
**Fill out the registration form**

<Steps>
<Step title="Enter personal details">
- **First Name**
- **Last Name**
</Step>

<Step title="Add contact information">
- **Work Email** address
- **Phone Number** with country code
</Step>

<Step title="Create your account">
- Click **"Start my free trial now"**
- You'll be redirected to the dashboard
</Step>

<Step title="Set up your password">
- You'll be prompted to create a password
- Enter your **Password**
- **Confirm Password**
- Click **"Create Account"**
</Step>

<Step title="Sign in">
- You'll be redirected to the login page
- Sign in with your email and password
</Step>
</Steps>
</Tab>

## Sign in to your account

Access your existing Tars workspace.

<Frame>
<img src="/images/login.png" alt="Tars login page showing email/password and social login options" />
</Frame>

### Login options

<Tabs>
<Tab title="Email & password">
**Traditional login**

- Enter your registered email
- Enter your password
- Click "Login"

**Forgot password?**

<Steps>
<Step title="Request password reset">
- Click "Forgot Password?" on login page
- Enter your email address
- Click "Send Reset Link"
</Step>

<Step title="Check your email">
- Look for magic link in your email
- Click the reset link
</Step>

<Step title="Create new password">
- You'll be redirected to password reset page
- Enter your **New Password**
- **Confirm New Password**
- Click "Reset Password"
</Step>
</Steps>
</Tab>

<Tab title="Social login">
**One-click access**

Use the same social account you signed up with:
- **Google** - Continue with Google
- **Outlook** - Continue with Outlook  
- **LinkedIn** - Continue with LinkedIn
- **SSO** - Continue with SSO
</Tab>
</Tabs>
</Tab>
