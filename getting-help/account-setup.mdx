### Signup options

<Tabs>
<Tab title="Manual signup">
**Fill out the registration form**

<Steps>
<Step title="Enter personal details">
- **First Name**
- **Last Name**
</Step>

<Step title="Add contact information">
  - **Work Email** address - **Phone Number** with country code
</Step>

<Step title="Create your account">
  - Click **"Start my free trial now"** - You'll be redirected to the dashboard
</Step>

<Step title="Set up your password">
  - You'll be prompted to create a password - Enter your **Password** -
  **Confirm Password** - Click **"Create Account"**
</Step>

<Step title="Sign in">
- You'll be redirected to the login page
- Sign in with your email and password
</Step>
</Steps>

### Login options

<Tabs>
<Tab title="Email & password">
**Traditional login**

- Enter your registered email
- Enter your password
- Click "Login"

**Forgot password?**

<Steps>
<Step title="Request password reset">
- Click "Forgot Password?" on login page
- Enter your email address
- Click "Send Reset Link"
</Step>

<Step title="Check your email">
  - Look for magic link in your email - Click the reset link
</Step>

<Step title="Create new password">
- You'll be redirected to password reset page
- Enter your **New Password**
- **Confirm New Password**
- Click "Reset Password"
</Step>
</Steps>
