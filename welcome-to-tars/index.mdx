## How Tars Works

### 1. Design with Gambits
Build conversation flows visually using our drag-and-drop **Gambit system**. No coding required.

**Key Gambits:**
- **AI Gambits**: Intent Detection, Data Extraction, Q&A, AI Agent
- **Input Gambits**: Text, Buttons, Cards, File Upload, Rating
- **Integration Gambits**: API calls, CRM actions, database operations

[Learn About Gambits →](/make/gambits-mastery/gambits-fundamentals/what-are-gambits)

### 2. Connect Your Data
Add **Knowledge Bases** from websites, documents, or structured data. Your agent intelligently retrieves accurate information.

[Explore Knowledge Bases →](/knowledge-bases)

### 3. Integrate Your Tools
Connect to **600+ tools** including HubSpot, Salesforce, Google Sheets, Zapier, and more. Automate actions across your tech stack.

[Browse Integrations →](/tools-integration)

### 4. Test & Optimize
Preview your agent in real-time, test different conversation paths, and use analytics to continuously improve.

[Testing Best Practices →](/test)

### 5. Deploy Everywhere
Launch on your website, WhatsApp Business, or standalone links. Custom domains supported.

[Deployment Options →](/deploy)

### 6. Analyze & Iterate
Track performance with detailed analytics. Understand user behavior and improve continuously.

[Analytics Guide →](/analyze)
