## Build Your First Agent in 15 Minutes

Follow this hands-on tutorial to create, configure, and deploy your first intelligent AI agent. No coding required!

### Step 1: Account Setup & Workspace

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="user-plus" title="Create your Tars account">
    1. Visit [https://tars.com/signup](https://tars.com/signup) to create your free account
    2. Verify your email address to activate your account
    3. Complete the onboarding wizard to set up your workspace
    
    <Tip>Choose your workspace name carefully - this will be used in your agent URLs!</Tip>
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="settings" title="Configure workspace settings">
    1. Set your default timezone and language preferences
    2. Choose your primary use case (Customer Support, Lead Generation, etc.)
    3. Invite team members if you're working collaboratively
    
    <Note>You can change these settings anytime from the workspace dashboard.</Note>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Step 2: Choose Your Starting Point

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="template" title="Select a template (Recommended)">
    1. Browse our collection of pre-built templates
    2. Choose based on your use case:
       - **Customer Support Bot** - Handle FAQs and support tickets
       - **Lead Qualification Agent** - Capture and qualify potential customers
       - **Booking Assistant** - Schedule appointments and meetings
       - **E-commerce Helper** - Product recommendations and order assistance
    3. Click "Use This Template" to start customizing
    
    <Tip>Templates include pre-configured conversation flows and best practices!</Tip>
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="hammer" title="Build from scratch (Advanced)">
    1. Click "Create New Agent" from your dashboard
    2. Choose "Blank Agent" to start with a clean slate
    3. You'll begin with the visual conversation builder
    
    <Warning>Building from scratch requires understanding of conversation design principles.</Warning>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Step 3: Basic Configuration

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion icon="robot" title="Set agent personality and behavior">
    1. **Agent Name**: Give your agent a memorable name
    2. **Description**: Explain what your agent does
    3. **Tone & Style**: Choose from professional, friendly, casual, or custom
    4. **Response Length**: Set preferred response length (concise, detailed, adaptive)
    
    <Tip>A well-defined personality makes conversations feel more natural!</Tip>
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="brain" title="Connect knowledge sources">
    1. **Website Scraping**: Add your website URL to import content automatically
    2. **Document Upload**: Upload PDFs, Word docs, or text files
    3. **Manual Entry**: Add specific FAQs and responses
    4. **Structured Data**: Import from CSV files or databases
    
    <Note>The more relevant information you provide, the better your agent's responses will be.</Note>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Step 4: Test Your Agent

<Accordion icon="flask" title="Test in real-time preview">
  1. Click the "Preview" button in the top-right corner
  2. Test different conversation scenarios:
     - Ask questions your customers might ask
     - Try edge cases and unexpected inputs
     - Verify the agent stays on topic
  3. Make adjustments based on testing results
  4. Use the "Reset Conversation" button to test different user journeys
  
  <Tip>Test early and often! Small issues become big problems if not caught early.</Tip>
</Accordion>

### Step 5: Deploy & Share

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="link" title="Get your agent link">
    1. Click "Deploy" from the agent dashboard
    2. Choose "Direct Link" for the quickest deployment
    3. Copy the generated URL to share with others
    4. Optionally customize the URL with your own domain
    
    <Tip>Direct links are perfect for testing and sharing with stakeholders!</Tip>
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="code" title="Embed on your website">
    1. Click "Get Embed Code" from the deployment options
    2. Copy the provided HTML snippet
    3. Paste it before the closing `</body>` tag on your website
    4. Customize the widget appearance and positioning
    
    <Note>The widget will appear as a chat bubble in the bottom-right corner by default.</Note>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
