# Your First AI Agent

Create, configure, and deploy your first intelligent AI agent in just **15 minutes**. This comprehensive tutorial guides you through the entire process with no coding experience required.

<Card title="What You'll Accomplish" icon="target" horizontal>
  By the end of this tutorial, you'll have a **fully functional AI agent**
  deployed and ready to engage with users. You'll learn core concepts and best
  practices for building intelligent conversations.
</Card>

## 15-Minute Quick Start

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Account">
    Sign up for Tars and get familiar with the platform through our guided tour.
    
    ### Quick Account Setup
    1. Visit [app.hellotars.com](https://app.hellotars.com) and create your account
    2. Choose from multiple signup options: email, Google, LinkedIn, Outlook, or SSO
    3. Verify your email address to activate your account
    4. Complete the initial workspace setup
    
    <Note>
      Need detailed signup help? Check our [complete account setup guide](/getting-help/account-setup) for step-by-step instructions and troubleshooting.
    </Note>
    
    ### Platform Tour (Recommended)
    Follow our interactive platform tour to create your first agent:
    - The tour guides you through the entire agent creation process
    - You'll have a fully functional agent by the end of the tour
    - Perfect for first-time users to understand the platform
    
    <Check>
      After completing the platform tour, you'll have your first agent created and ready to use.
    </Check>
    
    <Tip>
      The platform tour is the fastest way to get started - most users complete it in under 10 minutes.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

{" "}
<Step title="Create Your Agent">
  Choose how you want to build your agent using our flexible creation options.
  ### Agent Creation Methods Navigate to the **Agents** section and click
  **"Create Agent"**. Choose from these options:
  <Columns cols={2}>
    <Card title="Create from Template" icon="layout-template">
      **Fastest Option**: Choose from our template library with pre-built
      conversation flows
    </Card>
    <Card title="Create from Knowledge Base" icon="database">
      **Data-Driven**: Build an agent powered by your existing content and
      documents
    </Card>
    <Card title="Create from Tools" icon="wrench">
      **Integration-First**: Start with external tool integrations and build
      around them
    </Card>
    <Card title="Create from Scratch" icon="paintbrush">
      **Full Control**: Build completely custom conversation flows from the
      ground up
    </Card>
  </Columns>
  ### Template Selection (Recommended) If you choose "Create from Template": 1.
  Browse the **Template** section 2. Select a template that matches your use
  case 3. Click on any template to preview its conversation flow 4. The template
  will automatically load into your **Builder Canvas**
  <Check>
    Your selected template is now loaded in the Builder Canvas with all gambits
    and conversation flows ready to use.
  </Check>
  <Note>
    Templates include pre-configured gambits and conversation logic that you can
    customize to your needs.
  </Note>
</Step>

{" "}
<Step title="Customize Your Agent (Optional)">
  Use the Builder Canvas to review and customize your agent's conversation flow.
  ### Builder Canvas Overview In the Builder Canvas, you can see: - **Your
  agent** and all its settings - **All gambits** used in your template -
  **Conversation flow** and connections between gambits ### Customization
  Options
  <Columns cols={2}>
    <Card title="Configure Tab" icon="settings">
      **Agent Settings**: Customize chatbot appearance, behavior, and properties
    </Card>
    <Card title="Agent Prompt" icon="message-square">
      **AI Behavior**: Modify base prompts and LLM model selection
    </Card>
    <Card title="Knowledge Bases" icon="database">
      **Data Sources**: Add websites, documents, or CSV files to enhance
      responses
    </Card>
    <Card title="Advanced Properties" icon="wrench">
      **Chat Settings**: Tool integrations, events, and advanced configurations
    </Card>
  </Columns>
  ### Adding Knowledge Sources To enhance your agent's knowledge: 1. Navigate to
  the **Knowledge Bases** section 2. Choose from these data source options: -
  **Websites**: Automatically scrape website content - **Documents**: Upload
  PDFs, Word docs, text files - **CSV Files**: Import structured data - **Manual
  Entry**: Add custom questions and answers
  <Check>
    Your agent is now configured and ready for testing. You can always return to
    customize further.
  </Check>
  <Note>
    For your first agent, the template settings work well out-of-the-box. You
    can customize later based on testing results.
  </Note>
</Step>

{" "}
<Step title="Test Your Agent">
  Test your agent using the built-in preview functionality before going live.
  ### Preview Testing In the Builder Canvas: 1. Click the **"Preview"** button
  to test your agent in real-time 2. Try different conversation scenarios and
  user inputs 3. Verify responses match your expectations 4. Test the complete
  conversation flow ### Launch for External Testing For more comprehensive
  testing: 1. Click the **"Launch"** button in the Builder Canvas 2. Your agent
  opens in a new tab for full testing 3. Share this link with team members for
  feedback 4. Test on different devices and browsers ### Review Conversations
  After testing, analyze your agent's performance: - Visit the **Data View**
  section to see all conversations - Review user interactions and agent
  responses - Identify areas for improvement - Export conversation data if
  needed
  <Check>
    Test your agent with at least 5-10 different conversation scenarios to
    ensure it handles various user inputs appropriately.
  </Check>
  <Tip>
    The Preview and Launch features let you test immediately without deploying
    publicly. Use Launch for comprehensive testing with your team.
  </Tip>
</Step>

  <Step title="Publish & Deploy">
    Make your agent live and accessible to users through various deployment options.
    
    ### Publish Your Agent
    
    Once you're satisfied with testing:
    1. Click the **"Publish"** button in the Builder Canvas
    2. Your agent is now live and ready for deployment
    3. You'll receive confirmation that publishing was successful
    
    ### Deployment Options
    
    Choose how you want to make your agent accessible:
    
    <Columns cols={2}>
      <Card
        title="Website Embedding"
        icon="globe"
      >
        **Most Popular**: Embed as a chat widget on your website for ongoing customer engagement
      </Card>
      <Card
        title="WhatsApp Integration"
        icon="message-square"
      >
        **Mobile-First**: Deploy on WhatsApp Business for messaging app engagement
      </Card>
      <Card
        title="Direct Link Sharing"
        icon="link"
      >
        **Quick Setup**: Share a direct link via email, social media, or messaging
      </Card>
      <Card
        title="API Integration"
        icon="code"
      >
        **Custom Solutions**: Integrate with existing systems using our API
</Card>
    </Columns>
    
    ### Monitor Performance
    
    After deployment, track your agent's success:
    - **Data View**: Monitor all conversations and user interactions
    - **Analytics**: Review performance metrics and engagement rates
    - **Export Options**: Download conversation data for analysis
    - **Optimization**: Make improvements based on real user feedback
    
    <Check>
      Your agent is now published and deployed! Test the deployment to ensure it's working correctly for new users.
    </Check>
    
    <Note>
      You can always return to the Builder Canvas to make updates. Changes require republishing to take effect.
    </Note>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Congratulations!

You've successfully created and deployed your first Tars AI agent! Your agent is now live and ready to engage with users, providing intelligent responses and handling customer interactions 24/7.
